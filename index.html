Bungou Stray Dogs

Bungou Stray Dogs is an anime series where people who have powers called "Gifted" lives in a present day Japan society, Yokohama. Each day, there are stories, events that occur involving multiple organizations such as the infamous Port Mafia, The Armed Detective Agency, The Guild, and lots more. Each individual in each of the affiliation has their own unique powers, such as the ability to control gravity, the ability to create illuisons, transform into a weretiger, and a lot more gifted with their special powers for us to uncover.
